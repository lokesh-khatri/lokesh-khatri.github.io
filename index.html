About

At that time I didn't know much about Visual Basic but I always used to wonder what goes on inside the computer to make the program work. On that day a seed of curiosity was sown.

I want to become an integral part of this extraordinary world, contribute my efforts in every single way possible and be a part of the change computer industry is bringing into our lives.
